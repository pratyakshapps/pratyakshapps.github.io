Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Standards

We take child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) seriously and adhere to strict policies and guidelines to ensure the safety of all users. Our application follows a set of external standards and practices designed to combat CSAE and protect children from harm.

External Standards Against CSAE

Our application complies with the following external standards:

  • Internet Watch Foundation (IWF) - A global initiative to remove child sexual abuse material from the internet.
  • Stop It Now - Provides resources to prevent and stop child sexual abuse.
  • Thorn - A nonprofit organization dedicated to building technology to defend children from sexual abuse.

Reporting and Compliance

We provide a direct channel for reporting suspected cases of child sexual abuse and exploitation. Users can report incidents through the in-app reporting system, which is monitored by our moderation team. Additionally, our team works closely with relevant authorities to address any potential cases promptly.

Commitment to Safety

We are committed to ensuring that our platform is a safe environment for all users. This includes implementing robust age verification methods, monitoring content, and enforcing strict policies against abusive behavior.
